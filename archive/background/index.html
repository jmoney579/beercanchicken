About Me

My Childhood

My early childhood was the typical thing one would expect from a child of that age. Running around carelessly, getting into trouble and then repeating it the next day. Having little to no understanding of the real world and how it worked life was an adventure every day.

 

I was born and raised in Chesapeake, VA. For those not familiar with the area, the city is located just above the North Carolina-Virginia boarder in the Tidewater area with Virginia Beach, Norfolk and Suffolk surrounding it. Aside from going to mutiple different place in the area, I did relativly little traveling outside the area. This led me later on in life to seek out new places and develop a life long notion of trying anything once.
