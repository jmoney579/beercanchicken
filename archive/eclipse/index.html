Welcome


UNIQUE FUNCTIONS  - EASY TO USE - COST EFFICIENT

EGEMS is a user-friendly database for anyone who wishes to better manage their geological materials. This site is designed to introduce you to EGEMS and to help answer any questions you might have. If you can't easily find the answer to your question, please do not hesitate to contact ECLIPSE at customersupport@eclipseservices.biz. 



EGEMS was developed at Virginia Tech and it is currently being used by the University's Department of Geosciences, as well as the
Department's Museum.
