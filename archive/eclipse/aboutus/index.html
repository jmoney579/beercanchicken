CEO & Founder - Sarah Timm


Thank you for your interest in ECLIPSE! As developer and founder of EGEMS, my hope is that this software will enable better-preserved and more accessible geological collections. My own collection has been growing for years and has many different types of geological materials. Until I started using EGEMS though, even with yearly upkeep, I never knew where all of my materials were.
 

While completing my BA and MS degrees in geoscience, I saw how university and college geological departments constantly struggled to manage their collections. I also spent time with database software that was difficult to use and outdated. I developed EGEMS as a solution to these increasing needs.


Since I started using EGEMS, for the first time ever, my collection feels under control. If you would like your collection to be superbly managed and flawlessly searchable, then I can guarantee EGEMS is for you. I invite you to explore the website, and if you have any questions, please contact me or my collaborators directly.
