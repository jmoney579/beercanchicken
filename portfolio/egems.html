Electronic GeoManagement Software (EGEMS)

The Electronic Geological Management System(EGEMS) is a software package developed by ECLIPSE. This is ECLIPSE services' first production system eligible for institutional and private consumer purchase. I was hired to redesign their current site and develop a more professional appearance. This site incorporates multiple PHP based forms for contact and software purchases made by consumers. Using paypal's linklok system, we (my client and I) developed a secure purchasing site for product sales.


Update:

EGEMS has become a creative commons application and the owner can no support marketing of the product. The site has been retired; however, you can still view its design and functionality through the sample site below.
