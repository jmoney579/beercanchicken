Office of Newcomers Services

In Development

The primary mission of the Office of Newcomer's Services is to provide assistance to refugees in Virginia with their immigration into America and any legal issues, which may affect their consideration for residency. Currently they occupy a very small section of the main Virginia Department of Social Services website; however as refugee immigration has grown, so has their need for a more prominent web presence. Usining bootstrap as the intial framework, I developed the site and am currently in the process of loading the live content.
