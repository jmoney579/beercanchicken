Office of Newcomers Services

In Development

The primary mission of the Office of Newcomer's Services is to provide assistance to refugees in Virginia with their immigration into America and any legal issues, which may affect their consideration for residency. Currently they occupy a very small section of the main Virginia Department of Social Services website; however as refugee immigration has grown, so has their need for a more prominent web presence. This microsite is being built using bootstraps framework. I also developed the site and am in the process of loading the live content.

Throughout it's creation I've had a few major hurdles to cross. The first was the it's unique navigation and getting it to function correctly in a mobile state. This huddle is one that I'm still struggling with; however, I've devised a workaround until a more permanent solution can be put into place.

The second issue that developed was devising a way to import an RSS feed into the site which would essentially be a multi-site crawl of news stories about refugee resettlement. Importing the RSS feed was easy but finding a reliable product which would crawl news sites and create an RSS feed was not. In the end, we requested the services of Meltwater to perform this function.

The final issue is one that has been a struggle in every site that I've built and that is content development. Unfortunately, much of the content needed for the site didn't previously exist and without it the site cannot be launched. The initial launch date of the site was October 1st of last year, but due to this set back the site has yet to be launched. My contribution to the site, the design, coding and page templates was completed far before the deadline. Content has since been provided to me and I'm in the process of loading it into the site.
