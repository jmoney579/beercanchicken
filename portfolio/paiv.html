Public Assistance Investigators of Virginia

Recently Launched

The Public Assistance Investigators of Virginia (PAIV) is an organization comprised of multiple state, federal, public and private institutions. Their primary mission is to prevent, detect and adjudicate fraudulent activities which could lead to the unlawful receipt of public assistance.

Prior to my work, they lacked any sort of public web presence. Utilizing a bootstrap template and heavy modification, I was able to create a unique design for their organization in a relatively short development period. Unlike the majority of bootstrap powered site, this one employs a vertical navigation structure while still remaining responsive on multiple devices.
