Public Assistance Investigators of Virginia

In Development

The Public Assistance Investigators of Virginia (PAIV) is an organization comprised of multiple state, federal, public and private institutions. Their primary mission is to prevent, detect and adjudicate fraudulent activities which could lead to the illegal receipt of public assistance.

Prior to my work, they lacked any sort of public web presence. Utilizing a bootstrap template, which I heavily modified, I was able to create a unique design for their organization in a relatively short development period. Unlike the majority or bootstrap powered site, this one employs a vertical navigation structure while stile remaining responsive to multiple devices.
