Foster My Future

Recently Launched

Foster my future was a site created to support the Foster My Future campaign launched May 1st, 2017. This campaign sought to encourage individuals of all ages, nationalities and orientation of the importance of foster care. In this project, I served as project manager, coordinating the efforts of the web team and removing obstacles which would have prevented the team from completing the task by the project due date. Through the efforts of the web team and my guidance, we completed the site days before the project due date and, once we received the site content, launched the site in conjunction with the campaign announcement. The site is designed using a modular framework, since it is expected to grow. In it's initial state, as displayed, it serves as a single page site; however, it will eventually grow into one utilizing multiple pages
