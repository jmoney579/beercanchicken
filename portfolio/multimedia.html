Child Welfare Stipend Program Promotional Video

The Child Welfare Stipend Program is a program which was reintroduced in 2016 to assist current students in field of social work. The program provides a stipend for bachelor's and master's students to complete their education with the agreement to work at a local department of social services after graduation. This video was created for placement on the participant university websites to both promote and inform new students about the program. Interviews were conducted with multiple previous recipients of the stipend and b-roll was filmed at Virginia Commonwealth University. The graphical elements were designed using Adobe Photoshop and all editing was performed in Adobe Premier Pro.
