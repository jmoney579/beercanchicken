Easy to use & Cost efficient


EGEMS manages all of your geological materials with one, user friendly program. The combination of exclusive and standard features makes this the ideal software for anyone looking to preserve their geological materials and their associated data.


• Preserve important data, images, and documents


• Record specimen use and create dynamic graphs


• Track if a specimen has moved from it's home or was returned


• Relate research products (thin sections, experimental powders, synthetic samples, etc) to their original material


• Capture associated analytical data and publications


• Link maps and GPS coordinates to specimens


• Easily import/export in many formats (Excel, PDF, Jpg, etc)


• Use on a Mac or PC platform
