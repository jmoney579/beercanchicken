NLI Registration System Help

Where is the Computer Selection page?

Once you have logged into the enrollment system, you can navigate to these pages using the main navigation at the top of the page. You must be logged in to see this page.

Where are the Summer Program offerings?

You must be logged in to see the Summer Programs. If you are eligible to participate in these programs, you will see a Summer Programs selection on the Home page of the enrollment system. However, enrollment in Summer Programs is limited.

How do I view current NLI offerings?

Navigate to the enrollment system and browse or search for sessions.

How do I enroll in a session?

Browse or search for the session of your choice. Click the title of the session. Click the Enroll or Enroll Online button.

How do I un-enroll from a session?

Navigate to My Schedule, find the session title, click the corresponding UN-ENROLL button.

What is a theme?

A theme is a topic area within the NLI curriculum. Each session is associated with one or more themes. We currently have seven themes: Engaging Learners, Security Your Technology, Research & Discovery, Visual Media & Web Design, T.E.A.L Tools, Programming & Software, and Online Strategies & eLearning.

What is an online session?

An online session is a live webcast of the session. This option is made available, primarily, for participants who are not located in Blacksburg.

What is My Schedule?

My Schedule is a list of sessions in which you are enrolled, waitlisted, or scheduled to lead.

What is the Transcript?

The transcript is a history of all NLI sessions you have completed.

Where are My Schedule and Transcript?

Once you have logged into the system, you can navigate to these pages using the main navigation at the top of the page. You must be logged in to see these pages.

Who is eligible to participate in NLI offerings?

All current faculty, staff, and graduate students.

When are new sessions added to the list of offerings?

The bulk of the sessions are added by the start of the spring and fall semesters; however, sessions may be added throughout the academic year.

How do I see sessions I have attended or led?

Navigate to the Enrollment system and select Transcript from the main navigation menu.

How do I see the sessions in which I’m enrolled or scheduled to lead?

Navigate to the Enrollment system and select My Schedule.

Known issue details and resolutions/workarounds

White page after login

For users who have blocked browser cookies or have a cookie blocker installed, the browser may display a blank white page which appears to be loading after successful login to CAS(Central Authentication Services). Example below:

Workaround

If allowing browser cookies is not an option, proceed to the registration system and attempt to login by clicking the VT Login text. After successful login(blank white browser window loading indefinitely), replace the url in the address bar with http://app.nli.tlos.vt.edu. Following the previous step, click on the VT Login link to complete the login process.
