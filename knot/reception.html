Reception
Celebrating the union of Shweta & Jacques

Please join us for our wedding reception which celebrates this new and exciting phase in our lives. The reception will be American style but with delicious Indian food, and a cake designed by Jacques. We will also have cocktail hour before the reception. Be prepared to dance the night away!

PS - You can take advantage of the time between the ceremony & reception to explore downtown Richmond or simply to take a snooze. Indian weddings usually have this time gap so you have time to change your attire for the reception.

Date: Sept. 6, 2015 11:00am-01:00pm
Attire: Indian or Formal
Location: Richmond Marriott Grand Ballroom
500 E Broad St, Richmond, VA 23219
Parking: Parking Garage
