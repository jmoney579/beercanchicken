The Sangeet
Music, Dancing and मस्ती
Sept. 5, 2015

The literal translation of Sangeet is Music in Hindi. This is a night filled with lots of dancing and singing. Think of it as a pre-wedding reception or an upscale rehersal dinner. Traditionally, it only involved the women in the immediate family but we wanted to include everyone so that everyone can get in the wedding spirit.

Attire: Semi-formal, Indian

Date: Sept. 5, 2015 7:30pm-11:30pm {Dinner included}
Location: Deep Run Park Grand Ballroom - 9900 Ridgefield Pkwy, Henrico, VA 23233
Parking: Available on Site
